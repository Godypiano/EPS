Apply permit


Registered transporters/applicants can log in to the system anytime from anywhere to apply for permit.

  1. To apply permit, click to the Apply permit link at the left menu.

  1. Read the terms and Conditions and Click apply button as indicated below

  1. The permit application form will be displayed as show below.


Follow the following steps to fill the permit application form

  1. Fill in Vehicle and cargo details
    • Select type of vehicle
    • Select Vehicle lease type


  1. Applicant details will be filled automatically
  2. Fill in Load Information
    • Select Load Type
    • Select Load Category
    • Select Load Name        
    • Select Cargo Name
    • Enter Load/ cargo Description        
    • Enter Phone No.


  1. FIll in Journey details
    • Date of the Transport
    • Permit Expiry Date will be filled automatically after selecting date of transport


  1. Fill in Transport Information
    • Length (meters) for Unladen Vehicle, Load and Laden Vehicle
    • Height (meters) for Unladen Vehicle, Load and Laden Vehicle        
    • Width (meters) for Unladen Vehicle, Load and Laden Vehicle        
    • Mass ( in Tonne) for Unladen Vehicle, Load and Laden Vehicle
    • Overhang Rear (meters) for laden Vehicle                        
    • Overhang Front (motor vehicle)  (meters) for laden Vehicle                                
    • Overhang Right  (meters) for laden Vehicle                                
    • Overhang Left (meters) for laden Vehicle                        
    • Overhang Front (drawbar)  (meters) for laden Vehicle        


  1. Select vehicle Axle Configuration
  2. Fill in Vehicle Registration Details
    • Vehicle Registration No.        
    • Certificate No


  1. Fill in Axle Information
    • Axle Spacing (M)
    • Actual Axle Load (Kg)


  1. Fill in Route Information
    • FROM
    • TO
    • VIA


  1. Upload required Attachments:
    • Upload Horse registration card
    • Upload Hiring Agreement
    • Upload Cargo Images & Drawings
    • Upload Weigh Ticket from TANROADS


  1. Enter Captcha Code as shown in the screen

  1. Click submit button to submit form, or Cancel to clear fields.
