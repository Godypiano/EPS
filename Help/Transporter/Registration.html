Registration


To register to the EPS as Transporter follow the steps below.


A. Filling the registration form

  1. Click register link as indicated on the figure above. The following form will be displayed

  1. If the transporter type is Company you will be required to enter company name.
  2. Enter TIN Number
  3. Enter Vehicle Registration Number e.g. T. 447 BWG
  4. Enter name of a contact person
  5. Enter title/designation of that contact person
  6. Enter address of the contact person
  7. Enter City *        
  8. Enter Country
  9. Enter  Zip/Postal Code
  10. Enter Mobile e.g. 654 112 343.(do not start with Zero)
  11. Enter Land Phone
  12. Enter Email address e.g. john@mow.go.tz
  13. Then type the captcha code visible below the form as indicated below.

  1. Click Register button to save or Cancel button to clear all fields

When you click register button message will be sent to your email address for activation.

B. Confirm activation

  1. To confirm activation go to your email and open the message from MWT (Works).

  1. Click the activation link as indicated above
  2. The link will take you direct to the activation screen as shown below

  1. Enter password
  2. Confirm password and the Click ‘submit’ button, or ‘Reset’ to clear fields
  3. When you click ‘submit’, registration for transporter will be complete and the following message will display.
