Payments report

Payment report

Payment report has three categories of reports such as

  • Daily Report        
  • Monthly Report        
  • Invoice        

1. View Daily Report

Steps

  1. Go to Payment reports link under report menu
  2. Click daily report as shown below

  1. Enter range of time
    • Date from
    • Date to
  1. Click Run Report button to see the report
    • OR click download report by clicking ‘Download to pdf’ button


2. View Monthly Report

Steps

  1. Go to Payment reports link under report menu
  2. Click Monthly report as shown below

  1. Enter range of time
    • Month from
    • Month to
  1. Click Run Report button to see the report
    • OR click download report by clicking ‘Download to pdf’ button

3. View Invoice Report

Steps

  1. Go to Payment reports link under report menu
  2. Click Invoice report as shown below

  1. Enter range of time
    • Date from
    • Date to
  1. Click Run Report button to see the report
    • OR click download report by clicking ‘Download to pdf’ button
