    Introduction

    Introduction

    Electronic Special Load Permit System (EPS) is a system designed to provide permits to vehicles and loads on the road which are above permissible limit electronically. EPS automate all processes and transactions done to get a permit, from permit application to the issuing of the official permit that is sent through email. This system will involve more than one system for integration such as BOT, and RFB MIS. It will also produce reports for the Ministry of Work, Transport and Communication (Works) and other stakeholders.
